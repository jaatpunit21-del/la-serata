CLINICAL SPECIALITIES

Advanced Dental Specialities

Under the expert guidance of Dr. Parmod Tayal, our hospital provides specialized treatments in key areas of modern dentistry:

  • Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery: Expert management of complex extractions, jaw reconstructions, and facial trauma.
  • Maxillofacial Prosthetics: Restoring oral structures, rehabilitation of congenital or acquired defects.
  • Laser Dentistry: Painless soft tissue treatments, laser-assisted whitening, and precise surgeries.
  • Tooth Reshaping & Cosmetic Enhancements: Minimally invasive corrections for beautiful, balanced smiles.
