Dental FAQ

Read clear, medical answers to frequently asked questions about dental care and clinic procedures.

For most patients, we recommend visiting our dental clinic in Hisar twice a year (every six months) for a routine dental check-up and professional teeth cleaning. Regular examinations help in early detection of decay and gum issues.

Teeth whitening is a safe, effective cosmetic procedure designed to brighten your teeth by breaking down and lifting stains caused by foods, beverages, or aging. At Arogya Dental Hospital, we offer professional, enamel-safe whitening with minimal sensitivity.

Yes, dental implants are designed to be a permanent, lifetime tooth replacement solution. The titanium post fuses directly with your jawbone. While the custom ceramic crown on top may experience normal wear over many years and eventually require restoration, the implant base remains secure.

Treatment times depend entirely on the type and complexity of the dental care needed. Routine scaling takes about 30 to 45 minutes, cosmetic teeth whitening takes 60 minutes, and implants or orthodontic alignment are completed in stages over several months.

Yes, routine check-ups are essential even if your teeth feel fine. Many oral issues, like early cavities or gum inflammation, develop without pain. Catching them early at Arogya Dental Hospital prevents them from turning into painful, expensive treatments.

In a dental emergency (such as a knocked-out tooth, severe jaw pain, or intense bleeding), call our emergency number immediately at 088130 60169. Try to keep any dislodged tooth moist (in milk or saliva) and seek clinical care at our Hisar Malik Chowk clinic as soon as possible.
