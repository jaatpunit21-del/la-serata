COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our premium banquet halls, party lawn space, catering packages, and site policies.

What are the capacities of the different zones at La Serata? ▼

La Serata offers multiple distinct zones to match different guest counts:

  • The Grand Lawn: Hosts up to 800 guests, perfect for grand wedding receptions and large exhibitions.
  • The Royal Banquet Hall: Accommodates up to 300 guests, featuring a premium double-height ceiling and luxury chandeliers.
  • The Courtyard Garden: Holds up to 150 guests, ideal for sangeet nights, mehendi ceremonies, or cocktail gatherings.
  • VIP Bridal Suite & Groom Lounge: Private air-conditioned grooming areas for host preparation.
Where is the venue located and how is the parking? ▼

We are situated Behind Shreedhar Corporate House, Near Shilaj Circle, Sardar Patel Ring Road, Shilaj, Ahmedabad. The venue offers wide access gates directly off the ring road, with a dedicated parking lot supporting up to 200 vehicles along with professional valet services.

Do you provide in-house catering and what are the rates? ▼

Yes, we offer exquisite, customized in-house vegetarian catering featuring multi-cuisine selections (North Indian, Gujarati, Continental, Live Italian, and Fusion desserts). Pricing starts from ₹1,100 per plate for our Classic package, ₹1,500 for Royal Multi-Cuisine, and ₹1,800 for our Premium Signature Fusion package.

Are external catering teams or decorators permitted? ▼

To guarantee food safety and five-star quality standards, external catering is generally not permitted. Our expert chefs can tailor the menu to include specific regional cuisines. For decor, we work with a panel of premium approved decorators to ensure structural integrity and premium design aesthetics, though external designers may be approved on a case-by-case basis.

What is the booking and cancellation policy? ▼

To secure a specific date, a 25% non-refundable advance booking deposit is required. The remaining payment is divided into subsequent milestones: 50% paid 30 days prior to the event, and the remaining 25% settled 7 days before the event starts. Dates can be rescheduled free of charge if requested at least 90 days in advance, subject to slot availability.

Are site visits and walkthroughs open to the public? ▼

Yes, our reservation desk is open daily from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM for guided site walkthroughs and catering trials. We highly recommend booking an appointment via our contact form or calling our coordinator at +91 99256 75751 to ensure an event manager is available to assist you.

Do you provide power backup and sound system integration? ▼

Yes, La Serata is equipped with a 100% silent generator power backup system that supports full hall lighting and air conditioning. We also provide integrated sound wiring, premium acoustical setups in the Royal Banquet Hall, and support for external DJ setups on the lawn within local regulatory decibel guidelines.

Have a Custom Request?

If you have specific decoration themes, seating layouts, or custom dietary requests for your group booking, feel free to contact our events desk directly.
