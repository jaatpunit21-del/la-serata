COSMETIC BRIGHTENING

Professional Teeth Whitening

Over time, teeth can lose their natural brightness due to lifestyle habits, aging, or consuming staining food and beverages like tea, coffee, and spices. At Arogya Dental & Multispeciality Hospital, we offer safe, clinically supervised teeth whitening procedures designed to lift stubborn stains and significantly brighten your smile.

Unlike over-the-counter DIY kits that can irritate your gums or erode enamel, our professional whitening treatments use verified, enamel-safe bleaching agents and protective barriers to ensure comfort, minimum sensitivity, and long-lasting aesthetic improvements.

Benefits of Professional Whitening

  • Quick results in a single session (in-chair whitening)
  • Enamel-safe, scientifically formulated compounds
  • Precise coverage minimizing post-treatment sensitivity
  • Customized shade matching for a natural, bright finish

Our Whitening Process

1

Shade Assessment

We begin by evaluating your current tooth color using a standard shade guide and discussing your desired brightness goals.

2

Gum Protection

A protective gel barrier is carefully applied to your gums to keep them safe from the active whitening ingredients.

3

Whitening Gel Application

The specialized whitening gel is applied to the front of your teeth and activated, breaking down stains beneath the surface.

4

Rinse & Post-Care Guide

The gel is removed, revealing your brighter smile. We provide a post-treatment care guide to help maintain your results.

Expected Results

Patients typically notice teeth that are several shades lighter immediately following the session. The longevity of the whitening effect varies depending on oral hygiene habits and dietary choices. Avoid dark beverages and smoking immediately after treatment for optimal, lasting results.
