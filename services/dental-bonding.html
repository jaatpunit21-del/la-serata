Dental Bonding

Minor dental flaws like chips, small fractures, or slight gaps between teeth can affect your smile's symmetry. Dental bonding is a quick, highly effective, and non-invasive procedure designed to resolve these aesthetic concerns. At Arogya Dental & Multispeciality Hospital, we use composite resins that match the natural color of your enamel perfectly.

Because the composite resin bonds directly to the tooth surface, the procedure requires minimal prep work or removal of natural enamel, making it an excellent conservative treatment choice.

Key Benefits of Dental Bonding

  • Fast treatment, usually completed in a single short visit
  • Conservative procedure preserving maximum natural enamel
  • Seamless matching with neighboring teeth shades
  • Cost-effective solution for minor chips and gaps

The Bonding Procedure Steps

1

Shade Customisation

We select a composite resin shade that matches your natural teeth, ensuring a seamless, invisible repair.

2

Tooth Preparation

The tooth surface is lightly textured and a conditioning liquid is applied, helping the bonding agent adhere securely.

3

Resin Shaping & Curing

The soft resin is applied, shaped to match the contours of your tooth, and cured (hardened) using a specialized light.

4

Final Polish

Once hardened, the material is further trimmed, shaped, and polished to match the sheen of surrounding enamel.

Dental Bonding FAQs

Most bonding procedures take only 30 to 60 minutes per tooth, allowing you to walk out with a fully restored smile in a single visit.

In most cases, anesthesia is not needed unless the bonding is being used to fill a decayed cavity near sensitive nerve endings.
